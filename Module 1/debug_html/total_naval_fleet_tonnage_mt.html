Navy Fleet by Tonnage by Country (2026)



NAVAL POWER | FLEET STRENGTH | DISPLACEMENT | FIREPOWER

Global Navy fleets ranked by total tonnage.



Beginning in 2025, the annual GFP review tracks fleet firepower by total fleet tonnage (in tonnes) to provide for a more accurate reading of a naval force's realistic sea-going strength providing a more accurate reflection. This better delineates how a force fielding 100 gunboats cannot realistically equate to an opposing force fielding even single nuclear-powered aircraft carrier (itself housing 80 or more warplanes).

Data presented on this list is through 2026. For 2025, only 47 nations are featured in this ranking including many of the top fighting naval powers.
